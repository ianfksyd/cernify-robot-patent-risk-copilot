# Commercial Context

Version: UCWS public submission edition  
Status date: 2026-05-29

## Positioning

Cernify is a full-cycle AI patent platform centered on patent drafting. The robotics patent-risk demo is not a standalone patent-search wrapper; it is the product-evidence layer that prepares better inputs for drafting, FTO preparation, office-action response, and counsel review.

The commercial thesis is simple:

```text
better technical evidence -> stronger patent drafting workflow -> faster professional review -> reusable patent work product
```

## Users

Cernify is designed for professional patent and hardtech workflows:

- Patent attorneys and patent agents who need structured invention evidence before drafting.
- In-house IP teams preparing overseas product launch, supplier review, or portfolio planning.
- Robotics founders and product teams who need to explain technical features to counsel.
- Engineering teams that must separate observed product facts from assumptions and missing data.
- University technology-transfer teams and public IP service platforms that need repeatable disclosure and review workflows.
- Investors and diligence teams reviewing hardtech companies before financing or acquisition.

## Commercial Wedge

The first high-value workflow is patent drafting. Drafting creates a strong buying reason because users already spend time and budget on invention disclosure, claim preparation, drawings, review, export, and office-action follow-up.

Robotics FTO preparation is a natural adjacent workflow because it uses the same evidence discipline:

- What is the product?
- What technical features are visible or documented?
- Which features are patent-sensitive?
- Which assumptions are unsupported?
- What evidence is still missing before counsel can review?
- Which facts can become drafting-ready disclosure input?

## Product Lines

The broader Cernify platform can support several commercial product lines:

| Product line | Buyer | Value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Invention disclosure and patent drafting | Patent agents, law firms, inventors, enterprise IP teams | Turn technical materials into structured drafts and reviewable claim support. |
| Robotics and hardtech evidence package | Robotics teams, in-house IP, counsel | Organize product evidence, component risks, missing data, and review priorities before formal FTO work. |
| Office-action response preparation | Patent professionals and enterprise IP teams | Reuse drafting context, cited prior art, and claim structure for response workflows. |
| Patent portfolio and diligence review | Enterprises, investors, technology-transfer teams | Make technical claims, evidence strength, and missing support easier to inspect. |
| Team knowledge base and private deployment | Larger law firms, enterprises, public IP platforms | Preserve institutional knowledge, permissions, audit boundaries, and sensitive data controls. |

## Go-To-Market Path

The conservative commercialization path is:

```text
trial or demo -> first real task -> repeat usage -> paid pilot -> team workflow -> vertical package or private deployment
```

This matches the current stage of the company: working product depth is visible, while commercial conversion should be reported conservatively and only with evidence. Public UCWS materials should not overstate paid customers, signed contracts, government procurement, legal conclusions, or final professional opinions.

## Internationalization

The product is naturally international because patent drafting and launch risk cross jurisdictions. Cernify's broader platform is built around CNIPA, USPTO, EPO, and PCT-style workflows, while the UCWS robotics demo is framed around overseas expansion for US, EP, and JP review preparation.

International value comes from:

- Cross-jurisdiction drafting and document workflows.
- Structured translation and terminology discipline.
- Evidence packages that make product facts easier for foreign counsel to inspect.
- Robotics and hardtech use cases that global users can understand without local legal context.
- Public demo materials in English with clear legal and data boundaries.

## Differentiation

Cernify is different from keyword-only search tools because it starts from the product and the drafting workflow, not just from a query box. Search is one part of the patent workflow, but patent value depends on traceable technical evidence, claim support, figures, document quality, and professional review.

Cernify is also different from generic chatbots because the workflow is artifact-based:

```text
source material -> structured evidence -> claim or component support -> quality gate -> professional review artifact
```

For UCWS, the robotics demo makes that difference visible through a product-evidence workflow that can be understood quickly by global users and evaluated deeply by patent, product, and investment judges.

## Success Metrics

Useful product and business metrics include:

- Time from raw technical material to structured invention disclosure.
- Number of evidence gaps identified before drafting or counsel review.
- Claim-feature support coverage before export.
- Reduction in manual review loops for drawings, placeholders, unsupported effects, and terminology drift.
- Repeat usage by patent professionals or enterprise IP teams.
- Conversion from first task to paid pilot, team workflow, or private deployment.
